Am I Eligible to BE A TUTOR

ScholarBees doesn't mandate to have professional tutoring experience to be a tutor. If you feel passionate about tutoring and you think you have the right expertise, you can register to become a tutor.

Why ScholarBees?

ScholarBees provides the platform to reach thousands of learners online or in person.

Full Flexibility

At ScholarBees, you are in full control of your schedule. Teach when you like!

Several Modes of Tutoring

You can tutor online or in person. Not only you can tutor one-one-one but you can also create a learning series and let your students subscribe to it.

Be Discovered

You can leverage several tools to be discovered by your potentials students.

Set Your Fee
